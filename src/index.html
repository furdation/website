Moderation

Of course, like any other, our community has a few rules:

  • Be excellent to each other.
  • Do not spam external links.
  • Do not mass-invite other users.
  • Lewd content is not allowed within the rooms (extends to profile pictures and usernames).
  • In case of an issue, ping either an administrator or moderator to resolve it.
  • Because something isn't mentioned here doesn't means it's magically allowed, administrators and moderators have the final word.

Additionally, the room is synced to multiple ban lists:

  • Join #ftech-ban-list:j4.lc - Our own ban list, for people who broke the rules stated above.
  • Join #community-moderation-effort-bl:neko.dev - The largest anti-spam ban list on Matrix, aggregates multiple sources to ban spammers.
